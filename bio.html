Biography

Origin

I was originally born in New Jersey. However, when I was about 4 years old, my family moved to Luray, Virginia where I was raised for the rest of my life. While growing up, I had a difficult time learning English as a second language, considering that Gujarati (Indo-Aryan language) was my native language. By the time I was a junior in high school my family had to move to Winchester, Virginia. It was here where I received my Advanced Diploma from Millbrook High School.

Goals

In the future, I hope to work near the DC area where I could further my skills at a company that would engage in my critical thinking and problem-solving abilities.
